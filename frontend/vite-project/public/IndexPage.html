OUR SERVICES

About Us

Across the country campus housing has been available to students looking to increase their education, having the ability to focus on schooling, and live in a safe convenient location to classes. The best solution available to students is to provide on campus housing, unfortunately, the struggle is for college and universities to maintain a system that is easy for students and staff to manage, coordinate, and review dormitory operations. Therefore, it is important for campuses to capture information about student’s education level (freshman to senior), moving in, changing rooms, or requiring a roommate assignment.

Our Vision

It is our vision that a new system be developed which will provide staff and university administration the ability to easily manage and coordinate dormitories. Our design is a system which will allow the staff to coordinate room reservations with a web- based platform module that will allow students to create a reference number or send an email request to a friend or acquaintance they would like to room with, which could possibly allow the two students who know each other to become roommates. The requests will also include if the student has a room request based on accessibility situations, for example, a student in a wheel chair can request a handicapped room.
